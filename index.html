The Six Collections of Music Composition as Experience

Getting Started

Learn about the core ideas of teaching music composition for all types of learners, and introduce yourself to the community.

Music Works 1

Explore the progressive curriculum that can help every kind of learner compose original music. Try out new techniques for creating melody and harmony and share your thoughts and ideas with others. Take your students from beginning to end of their very first composition.

Music Works 2

Explore and develop new strategies for helping young composers build harmonic awareness in their music. Extend earlier principles of consonance and dissonance to apply to chord progressions.

Style

Through creating music, young composers gain new tools for exploring the world of music around them. Explore and create techniques that will help your students immerse themselves in a style of their choosing.

Extensions

There are many possible extensions that can grow from the YCIW curriculum. Explore many of the other composition projects that our community has created.

Reimagine Music Education

Apply the ideas of MCE to your own work, and share it with the community.
